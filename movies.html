Spiderman Movies

Explore the cinematic adventures of your friendly neighborhood Spiderman!

Major Spiderman Movies

Spider-Man (2002)

Director: Sam Raimi
Stars: Tobey Maguire, Kirsten Dunst
Plot: Peter Parker becomes Spiderman and faces the Green Goblin.

Spider-Man 2 (2004)

Director: Sam Raimi
Stars: Tobey Maguire, Alfred Molina
Plot: Spiderman battles Doctor Octopus while struggling with his own powers.

Spider-Man 3 (2007)

Director: Sam Raimi
Stars: Tobey Maguire, Thomas Haden Church
Plot: Peter faces Sandman, Venom, and his own dark side.

The Amazing Spider-Man (2012)

Director: Marc Webb
Stars: Andrew Garfield, Emma Stone
Plot: A new origin story with the Lizard as the main villain.

The Amazing Spider-Man 2 (2014)

Director: Marc Webb
Stars: Andrew Garfield, Jamie Foxx
Plot: Spiderman faces Electro and the loss of Gwen Stacy.

Spider-Man: Homecoming (2017)

Director: Jon Watts
Stars: Tom Holland, Michael Keaton
Plot: Peter Parker balances high school life and superhero duties.

Spider-Man: Far From Home (2019)

Director: Jon Watts
Stars: Tom Holland, Jake Gyllenhaal
Plot: Peter faces Mysterio on a European trip.

Spider-Man: No Way Home (2021)

Director: Jon Watts
Stars: Tom Holland, Zendaya
Plot: The multiverse brings together three generations of Spiderman.

Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse (2018)

Directors: Bob Persichetti, Peter Ramsey, Rodney Rothman
Plot: Miles Morales discovers the Spider-Verse.

Spider-Man: Across the Spider-Verse (2023)

Directors: Joaquim Dos Santos, Kemp Powers, Justin K. Thompson
Plot: Miles Morales continues his journey through the multiverse.
