About This Site

This website is a fan project dedicated to Spiderman, one of the most iconic superheroes in pop culture. Here you can explore his movies, games, comic stories, and more, all in one place.

Who is Spiderman?

Spiderman, created by Stan Lee and Steve Ditko, first appeared in Amazing Fantasy #15 in 1962. The story of Peter Parker, a young man who gains spider-like abilities, has inspired generations through comics, movies, games, and TV shows.
