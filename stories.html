Spiderman Stories

Legendary comic arcs that shaped Spiderman's legacy.

Famous Spiderman Comic Arcs

Amazing Fantasy #15 (1962)

Creators: Stan Lee, Steve Ditko
Summary: The first appearance of Spiderman, introducing Peter Parker and his origin story.

The Amazing Spider-Man #1 (1963)

Creators: Stan Lee, Steve Ditko
Summary: Spiderman gets his own series, facing the Fantastic Four and the Chameleon.

The Night Gwen Stacy Died (1973)

Writer: Gerry Conway
Summary: The tragic loss of Gwen Stacy at the hands of the Green Goblin.

Kraven's Last Hunt (1987)

Writer: J.M. DeMatteis
Summary: Kraven the Hunter defeats Spiderman and takes his place, leading to a dark psychological battle.

The Clone Saga (1994-1996)

Writers: Various
Summary: Peter Parker faces Ben Reilly, his clone, in a saga that questions his identity.

Spider-Verse (2014-2015)

Writer: Dan Slott
Summary: Spidermen from across the multiverse unite to battle Morlun and the Inheritors.
